HOUNSLOW CODE CLUB

About Us

Hounslow Code Club is based in Hounslow Library.It runs from 2-3 P.M. every Sunday.Children from age 7 to 13 years old learn the basics of programming. Different ideas are implemented using programming skills. Members showcase their work on their Fun Club Magazine website which provides us a platform to learn and share our work with everyone and inspire other children.Everyweek some new topics are curated and posted which are related to education,entertainment and general knowledge.
